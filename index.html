How to Search

Name: Enter the name of a Pokémon (e.g., Pikachu, Bulbasaur,..) to search for a specific Pokémon.

Type: Enter the type of Pokémon (e.g., Water, Fire, Grass,ghost,..) to search for Pokémon of that type.
